League and Conference Information

The University of Miami Club Lacrosse Team is a D2 member of the Men's Club Lacosse Association (MCLA) and the Southeastern Lacrosse Conference (SELC).

We play in the South division of the SELC which includes Florida Gulf Coast University, Florida Atlantic University and Florida Polytechnic University
